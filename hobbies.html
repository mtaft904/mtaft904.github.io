Hobbies!

Traveling

I hope to visit as many places as I can in my lifetime. So far I've visited 28 countries and lived in 3. In Spring 2022, I spent a semester abroad in Berlin and had the wonderful opportunity to be completely immersed in the culture and greatly improve my German skills. I hope to live abroad again in the future and learn more languages!

Baking

I got into baking over the pandemic, when I had lots of free time at home. Some of my favorite things to bake are cheesecake and tiramisu, although I'm always open to new recipes.

Art

I like to experiment in many different art forms and try various new projects, although I am not an expert in any of them. Over the years, I've taken a stab at mediums such as sewing, mural painting, digital drawing, knitting, calligraphy, origami, and many more. I am always ready to learn something new and create something cool!
